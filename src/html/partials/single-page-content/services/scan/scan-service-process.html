How We Work

  1. The client sends the Point Cloud data.

    Powerkh works with data captured by most common laser scanners (Trimble, Reigl, Leica Geosystems, FARO).

  2. We create a scope of work.

    Our teams will discuss the project’s requirements, specifications and define the Level of Development (LOD).

  3. We import your scan to Revit.

    We will transfer your Point Cloud files to Revit and ensure correct object positioning to reduce redundant data.

  4. Our team creates the BIM model.

    Powerkh refines raw data into a 3D BIM model with architectural, structural, and MEP systems.

  5. The client receives a 3D model & 2D drawings.

    You’ll get an accurate interactive 3D database and, optionally, shop drawings based on the BIM model.
