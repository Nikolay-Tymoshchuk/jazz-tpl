What Is BIM Software?

Building information modeling software goes beyond a particular computer program. Rather, it’s a set of digital products that enables AEC (architecture, engineering, and construction) professionals to put BIM into practice. Using BIM programs and tools, you can:

  • Generate, view, and manipulate an interactive 3D model that represents the physical and functional characteristics of a future facility
  • Share this 3D model with everyone involved in the building process — from architects and MEP engineers to supplies and clients — enabling them to work on it at the same time
  • Test your model under various conditions, such as predicting wind loads, ensuring pedestrian comfort, ensuring air quality, preventing contamination, etc.
  • Simulate the construction process to make exact estimations on time, materials, manpower and avoid clashes in processes

Please keep in mind that these are just a few examples of what BIM software can do. Still, some of these features, like 3D modeling and collaboration capabilities, are a must for the successful implementation of BIM practices.
