Scan to BIM Services

3D BIM models must represent all elements and systems accurately to reduce errors and improve turnaround time.

Many BIM modeling companies create interactive 3D models from flat drawings. However, Powerkh’s Point Cloud to BIM modeling services can help you achieve more precise results.

Point Cloud refers to data obtained from scanning three-dimensional objects. But the raw data is useless for 3D modeling. Our company, however, can transform your Point Cloud data into 3D as-built models for any construction project.

Our Scan to BIM services will allow you to get:

  • Highly accurate 3D BIM models for in-depth constructability reviews
  • Improved turnaround time for your BIM project
  • Better team collaboration via a unified cloud visualization platform
  • Variety of application possibilities (clash detection, refurbishment, building management)

How We Work

  1. The client sends the Point Cloud data.

    Powerkh works with data captured by most common laser scanners (Trimble, Reigl, Leica Geosystems, FARO).

  2. We create a scope of work.

    Our teams will discuss the project’s requirements, specifications and define the Level of Development (LOD).

  3. We import your scan to Revit.

    We will transfer your Point Cloud files to Revit and ensure correct object positioning to reduce redundant data.

  4. Our team creates the BIM model.

    Powerkh refines raw data into a 3D BIM model with architectural, structural, and MEP systems.

  5. The client receives a 3D model & 2D drawings.

    You’ll get an accurate interactive 3D database and, optionally, shop drawings based on the BIM model.
